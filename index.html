Answers

Frequently asked questions.

What happens if the machine breaks down during a morning rush?

You never wait days. We guarantee a 2-hour emergency response anywhere in Kyiv and Kyiv Oblast. Local, Ukrainian-speaking technicians arrive with the spare parts to bring your Swiss super-automatic back online immediately — preventing catastrophic revenue loss.

Do we have to buy a minimum amount of beans every month?

No. Unlike legacy roasters that force 15–30kg monthly quotas to pay off their "free" equipment, Anima never forces quotas. Our flat-rate service covers your infrastructure, so you never suffer cash-flow drag from stockpiling stale beans.

Are the machines protected against blackouts and voltage spikes?

Yes. Running 2,500W machines on diesel generators and unstable grids destroys delicate PCBs. We install industrial voltage stabilizers with every machine to buffer against lethal spikes and unbudgeted repair bills.

Our office water is highly mineralized. Will this ruin the machine?

Not with our system. Kyiv baseline water hardness is 124 mg/L, which causes rapid, destructive calcification. We conduct an upfront hydro-geological audit and install specialized, localized water filtration. Combined with included monthly calibration and descaling, your machine remains pristine.

Do we need to hire an experienced barista to operate the equipment?

No. The current labor shortage makes retaining skilled baristas nearly impossible. We deploy premium Swiss super-automatic machines (Franke, WMF) and include a mandatory 2-hour staff training program. Your cashiers or office managers execute specialty-grade coffee with the push of a button.

What exactly is in the flat monthly invoice?

Everything for continuous operation: Swiss equipment rental, premium origin-traced beans, 24/7 technical support, the 2-hour emergency SLA, mandatory staff training and monthly localized water calibration. Zero surprise line items or call-out fees.

Why shouldn't we just buy a $5,000 espresso machine outright?

Buying outright drains $3,000–$15,000+ in critical operating capital. Worse, when an owned machine breaks you pay out of pocket for emergency repairs, suffer days of downtime, and bear the entire liability of grid and water damage. Our managed service is total operational insurance.

What are your standard packages?

Our core B2B managed offering maps to the Growth Bundle at $1,997/mo. For multi-location retail or high-volume corporate integrations, we provide custom Enterprise quotes starting at $10,000+.
