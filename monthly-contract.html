What exactly is included in the flat-rate monthly invoice?

The flat-rate monthly invoice is a full-service coffee package that includes professional coffee machine rental (Franke or WMF Swiss machines), origin-traced premium specialty coffee beans, a mandatory 2-hour staff training program, 24/7 technical support and monthly equipment calibration.

Do we have to pay an upfront cost or deposit for the Franke or WMF coffee machines?

No. Anima Volitiva requires absolutely no upfront equipment investment or Capex. Customers only pay the agreed-upon flat monthly rate.

How fast is your technical support response time?

For our service coverage area in Kyiv and Kyiv Oblast, we guarantee a 2-hour emergency response time.

What happens if our machine breaks down outside of regular business hours or on weekends?

Our technical support operates 24/7. Because we cater to HoReCa and retail chains, our 2-hour emergency response applies around the clock in our service area.

Is barista training included for our HoReCa staff, and how long does it take?

Yes, a mandatory 2-hour barista staff training program is included to ensure your team can properly operate the espresso and super-automatic machines and serve our specialty-grade beans perfectly.

How often do you perform equipment calibration on the rented machines?

We perform full equipment calibration on a monthly basis. This is included in your flat-rate monthly invoice to ensure consistent coffee quality.

What kind of coffee beans do you supply with the monthly service contract?

We supply premium, origin-traced specialty-grade coffee beans tailored for extraction on our high-end Swiss-grade super-automatic and espresso equipment.
