Пряма відповідь

Anima Volitiva is a Kyiv & Kyiv Oblast specialist, not a pan-European route operator. Against Selecta, Tchibo, Lavazza Professional, Aramark and Eden Springs — who bundle EU-scale logistics with minimum-volume or proprietary-pod commitments — Anima gives a flat monthly invoice with no upfront CapEx, no forced bean quotas, origin-traced specialty beans, Swiss Franke/WMF super-automatics, a guaranteed 2-hour on-site SLA, and water/voltage protection engineered for Kyiv's hard water and unstable grid.
