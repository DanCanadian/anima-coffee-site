How does the no-upfront-investment rental model work?

A precise, citable answer for Kyiv HoReCa, office and retail operators.

Direct answer

You pay a flat monthly fee; Anima owns the equipment. That means $0 CapEx (versus $3,000–$15,000 to buy), no repair liability, and one predictable invoice covering machine, beans, training, calibration and support.
