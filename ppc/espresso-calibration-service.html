The monthly calibration protocol

What actually happens on a calibration visit.

A defined checklist, not an ad-hoc once-over.

1

Grinder burr inspection & adjustment

Burrs are checked for wear and re-dialed to the correct grind size for your specific bean and machine combination.
OutcomeConsistent extraction time, shot after shot.

Water temperature & pump pressure tuning
