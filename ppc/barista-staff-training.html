FAQ

Barista & staff training — answered.

Is training mandatory or optional?

Mandatory. Every staff member who will operate the machine completes the 2-hour program as part of deployment, and it's included in the flat monthly rate.

What exactly does the session cover?

Precision operation, daily cleaning and descaling protocols, POS billing procedures, error-code recognition, and the safe power-transition sequence for blackouts.

How much does it cut bean waste?

Sites report specialty bean waste dropping under 2% once staff are trained, versus significant losses from trial-and-error brewing beforehand.

Does training protect the machine itself?

Yes — a large share of catastrophic hardware failures trace back to untrained staff panic-resetting a machine during a power transition. Training eliminates that specific failure mode.

Do you retrain when staff turn over?

Yes. As part of the ongoing relationship we retrain new hires so quality never depends on any one employee's tenure.
