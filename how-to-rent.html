Answers

Rental questions.

What happens if the rented coffee machine breaks down during a busy shift?

We provide a guaranteed SLA with 24/7 rapid-response dispatch within Kyiv city limits. If the machine needs extensive repairs and can't be fixed on-site immediately, we deploy a loaner machine so your business experiences zero commercial downtime and never loses a sale.

Are preventative maintenance, decalcification and water-filter replacements included?

Yes. Unlike competitors who only react to complete breakdowns, our ecosystem includes proactive, scheduled decalcification, regular group-head gasket replacements and boiler-pressure checks to ensure consistent drink quality and prevent scaling.

If my business outgrows the machine, can we upgrade, and how does that affect the contract?

Absolutely. We base deployments on consultative sizing. If foot traffic scales or office headcount doubles, we seamlessly upgrade you from a 1-group to a 2 or 3-group machine, or to a super-automatic handling 200+ cups/day, simply by adjusting your monthly volume commitments.

Do we have to buy beans exclusively from you, and what are the minimum volumes?

Yes — our free-rental model is triggered by wholesale coffee commitments. Standard basic setups require 4 kg to 15 kg per month. Full professional sets (machine plus hopper grinder) require a baseline of 20 kg of roasted coffee per month.

Is installation, water-line connection and barista training provided on delivery?

Yes. Our single-invoice ecosystem includes comprehensive initial installation, connecting the machine and premium water filtration to your lines, and immediate barista training so your staff pours perfectly from day one.

What is the minimum commitment for a professional multi-boiler machine?

Commitments are tailored to the exact configuration — such as multi-boilers with 1.8 to 20-litre tanks — and are bound by monthly roasted-coffee volume metrics (for example, 20 kg for professional sets).

How quickly can a technician reach us in Kyiv on total equipment failure?

Our dedicated service hotline is available 24/7. We guarantee rapid response times within Kyiv city limits so you're never left facing extended downtime — halting sales while still paying rent and staff wages.
