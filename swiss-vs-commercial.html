Answers

Frequently asked questions.

What exactly is included in the flat-rate monthly invoice?

Your flat-rate monthly invoice is a comprehensive, zero-headache package. It entirely covers the rental of a premium Swiss-grade Franke or WMF super-automatic machine, a customized supply of our origin-traced specialty-grade beans, 24/7 technical support, monthly equipment calibration, and the mandatory 2-hour staff training.

Do we have to pay any upfront capital to install the Franke or WMF Swiss machines?

Absolutely not. Anima Volitiva operates on a strict zero upfront equipment investment model. You pay nothing to have these premium commercial espresso machines installed in your venue; your only cost is the predictable flat-rate monthly invoice.

How quickly will technical support arrive if our machine breaks down in Kyiv Oblast?

We guarantee a strict 2-hour emergency response time for all clients within our Kyiv and Kyiv Oblast service coverage area. Our 24/7 technical support infrastructure ensures you never lose significant revenue to prolonged machine downtime.

What is covered in the mandatory 2-hour barista staff training program?

The 2-hour training program ensures your staff understands the precise operation, daily cleaning protocols, and basic troubleshooting of the Swiss-grade equipment. This guarantees they can consistently extract the perfect cup from our origin-traced specialty beans without damaging the machinery.

Are the origin-traced premium specialty coffee beans included in the flat-rate package?

Yes. You do not need to source your own coffee. Our flat-rate monthly invoice includes a consistent supply of premium, origin-traced specialty-grade beans specifically calibrated to perform flawlessly in your designated Franke or WMF machine.

How does a Swiss super-automatic machine compare to a traditional commercial espresso machine for HoReCa?

Swiss super-automatic machines, like the Franke and WMF models we provide, remove user error from the equation. They deliver consistent, specialty-grade extraction at high volumes without requiring an expert barista on staff, saving you significant labor costs while maintaining premium quality.

What happens during the included monthly equipment calibration?

During our scheduled monthly visits, a trained technician inspects the machine, adjusts the internal grinders, calibrates the water temperature and pressure, and ensures the extraction parameters perfectly match the specific profile of the origin-traced specialty beans you are currently serving.

Is your 24/7 technical support available for all businesses in the Kyiv and Kyiv Oblast coverage area?

Yes. If your business is located within our defined service coverage area of Kyiv and Kyiv Oblast, you are fully covered by our 24/7 technical support network and the guaranteed 2-hour emergency response time.
